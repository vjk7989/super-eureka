# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Plantation operations leaders, farm managers, field inspectors, AI/dev reviewers, and auditors working across oil palm farms, blocks, tree-level disease detections, and ground inspection workflows.

## Product Purpose

Oil Palm Health Command Center helps teams find, prioritize, inspect, and track Ganoderma disease risk across large oil palm operations. Success means managers can locate affected farms and blocks quickly, inspectors can act on exact tree locations, and AI/dev teams can learn from false-positive and correction feedback.

## Brand Personality

Operational, precise, grounded.

## Anti-references

Do not make this feel like a marketing landing page, a decorative agritech demo, or a generic map toy. Avoid official Godrej logos or full brand claims unless approved assets are provided.

## Design Principles

- Map first, because location is the primary operating surface.
- Show severity and responsibility together, so teams know both what is affected and who owns the response.
- Keep dense information scannable for repeated operational use.
- Preserve a clear path from demo data to real GIS, Supabase, and PostGIS data.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-usable controls, non-color-only disease indicators, readable mobile inspector workflows, and reduced reliance on animation.
